The Beginning

Today the origin of the universe is explained by the Big Bang, in Greek Mythology everything began out of Chaos. As with many aspects of myth, it is uncertain what Chaos is but it is from this that the Primordial Deities emerged. It is from these beings that all Greek gods & heroes originate from.
