The Titans

Gaia’s Revenge

Gaia loved the Hecatoncheires but when Ouranos saw them with their hundred hands and fifty heads he was disgusted at how monstrous they were. They were the most powerful beings yet and Ouranos feared that they would grow up and threaten his supreme rule thus he cast them to the depths of Tartarus. Gaia was furious and urged all of her children in turn to help her get revenge. All refused to help her, all except for Kronos, the youngest and most fearless of the Titans.

Kronos laid in wait with a giant sickle created for him by Gaia. Later that night when Ouranos came to copulate with Gaia, he castrated his father and cast his genitals into the ocean. When they came into contact with the ocean, the water began to froth and foam and the Goddess of Beauty, Aphrodite, emerged. With Ouranos now powerless, Kronos and his sister Rhea took over from their father king and queen of the world. The Titans, were now the most powerful beings in the cosmos.
