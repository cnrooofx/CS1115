The Titans

Gaia’s Revenge

Gaia loved the Hecatoncheires but when Ouranos saw them with their hundred hands and fifty heads he was disgusted at how monstrous they were. They were the most powerful beings yet and Ouranos feared that they would grow up and threaten his supreme rule thus he cast them to the depths of Tartarus. Gaia was furious and asked all of her children in turn to help her get revenge. All refused to help her, all except for Kronos, the youngest and most fearless of the Titans.
